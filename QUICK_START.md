# 🚀 QUICK START - Deploy in 3 Steps!

## ⚡ Super Fast Setup (3 Minutes)

### Step 1: Go to Vercel
👉 **https://vercel.com/new**

### Step 2: Import Your GitHub Repo
- Click "Import Project"
- Paste: `https://github.com/anoopdusadh/trip-project`
- Click "Continue"

### Step 3: Deploy!
- Click "Deploy"
- **Wait 2-3 minutes...**
- ✅ Done! Your URL will appear

---

## 🎉 Your App is LIVE!

Your app will be available at:
```
https://trip-project.vercel.app
```
(or the custom URL shown in Vercel)

---

## 📱 Test Your App:

1. Open the live URL
2. Search for your location (autocomplete enabled)
3. Add places to visit
4. Click "SUBMIT & OPTIMIZE ROUTE"
5. See the optimal route! 🗺️
